# Application / enterprise developer

## Who

Builds and ships applications on internal platforms — services, CI/CD, and day-to-day coding. Expects golden paths, self-service access to tools/infra, and enough logs/traces to diagnose app issues without becoming a platform expert. Security scanning and “don’t make me architect common solutions from scratch” matter; AI/ML model work is not the primary job (use `ml-engineer` for that).

## When to use

- Audience is application developers, enterprise developers, or internal platform consumers
- Flows involving developer portals, sandboxes, onboarding, service catalogs, or app deploy/debug
- Platform engineering outcomes framed from the developer (tenant) side rather than the platform team
