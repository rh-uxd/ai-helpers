# Automation developer

## Who

Writes and maintains automation content — jobs, roles, inventories, and templates. Learning curve often centers on project structure, variables, and how the automation system fits together before fluent content writing. Values progressive examples and real job-task oriented learning; may come from other config-management or IaC tools, or from Windows/Linux ops with uneven YAML/Python prerequisites.

## When to use

- Audience is automation developers, content creators, or automation authors
- Flows involving writing/editing automation content, shared libraries, or runtimes
- Learning, time-to-value, or developer-experience surfaces for automation tools
