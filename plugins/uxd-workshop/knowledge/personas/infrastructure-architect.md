# Infrastructure architect

## Who

Designs and stewards infrastructure for the business — version compatibility, uptime, and keeping systems dependable without unnecessary cost. Thinks in standards, impact analysis, and change windows more than click-ops. Cautious about patch and major-version churn that breaks third-party stacks; wants a single management pane and advance notice of what will change.

## When to use

- Audience is infrastructure architects, solutions architects, or principal architects
- Flows involving capacity planning, architecture standards, multi-OS estates, or change impact
- When research or requirements distinguish architects from hands-on system admins or platform engineers
