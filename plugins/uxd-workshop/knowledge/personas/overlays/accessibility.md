# Accessibility

Inclusive-interaction context — not a standalone persona. Always layer onto a role persona; do not run as the only evaluator.

## Lens

Relies on keyboard navigation, assistive tech, and clear structure. Surfaces missing labels, focus traps, and ambiguous controls that a role lens alone may miss.

## When to apply

- Explicit a11y goals, or high-stakes interactive flows that should be inclusive
- Any walkthrough where keyboard / AT coverage matters alongside the primary role
