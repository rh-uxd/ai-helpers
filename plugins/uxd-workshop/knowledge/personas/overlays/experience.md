# Experience level

How long someone has done the work — not a standalone persona. Apply on top of any role persona.

## Lens

Shapes guidance tolerance, jargon comfort, and how aggressively someone explores the UI. Prefer one junior + one senior/experienced level when a persona lists multiple `default_experience` values.

## Levels

| Level | Tendency |
|-------|----------|
| Junior | Needs clearer labels, more guidance, lower tolerance for unfamiliar jargon |
| Senior | Faster navigation; notices missing power-user affordances and incomplete technical depth |
| Experienced | Operational fluency; expects efficiency, reliability cues, and safe day-2 actions |

## When to apply

- Almost every eval that uses a role persona (compose from `default_experience` when set)
- Comparing friction range across competence levels for the same role
