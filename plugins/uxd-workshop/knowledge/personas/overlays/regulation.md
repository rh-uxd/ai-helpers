# Regulated environment

Compliance / constrained-environment context — not a standalone persona. Always layer onto a role persona; regulation is a situation, not a job role.

## Lens

Distrusts opaque automation, missing provenance, and flows that assume unrestricted internet or cloud services. Looks for approvals, audit trails, offline constraints, and policy gates.

## When to apply

- Audience mentions regulated, air-gapped, FedRAMP, or strict change control
- Flows involving approvals, audit logs, offline constraints, or policy gates
