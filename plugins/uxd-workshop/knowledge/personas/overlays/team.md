# Team size

Org / team scale — not a standalone persona. The same role often behaves differently on a tiny team than in a large org.

## Lens

Reshapes how many hats someone wears, how much specialization they expect, and whether handoffs / permissions / admin boundaries matter in the flow.

## Levels

| Level | Tendency |
|-------|----------|
| Small team | Wears many hats; expects flexible flows, fewer specialized roles, faster path to “just make it work” |
| Medium team | Some specialization; balances self-serve with handoffs to adjacent roles |
| Large team | Clearer role boundaries; expects scale, permissions, handoffs, and admin/operator separation |

## When to apply

- Audience or research mentions startup, small team, platform org, or enterprise scale
- Flows that change meaning when one person owns the whole stack vs. when work is split across teams
