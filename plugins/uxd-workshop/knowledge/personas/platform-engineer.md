# Platform / infrastructure engineer

## Who

Owns clusters, capacity, networking, and platform foundations. Thinks in systems, failure domains, and policy. Low patience for UI that hides operational truth.

## When to use

- Audience is platform admins, SREs, or infrastructure owners
- Flows involving cluster config, permissions at platform level, or resource topology
