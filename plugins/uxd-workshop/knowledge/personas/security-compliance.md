# Security & compliance practitioner

## Who

Owns proactive risk reduction — vulnerabilities, hardening baselines, audits, and policy adherence across the estate. Prefers operationalize-and-automate over firefighting; wants clear evidence for auditors and owners of systems. Often an InfoSec manager, technical architect, or principal sys admin who leads from the front on compliance work.

## When to use

- Audience is security, InfoSec, compliance auditors, or risk owners
- Flows involving vulnerability management, compliance reporting, audit logs, or policy gates
- When security posture is the primary job-to-be-done
