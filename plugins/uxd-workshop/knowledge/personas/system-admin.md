# System administrator

## Who

Owns day-to-day care of servers and OS fleets — patching, imaging, vulnerability remediation, and keeping systems reliable. Often wears multiple hats (network, help desk, infrastructure) especially in smaller orgs. Titles vary widely; the job is keeping the lights on with limited time for tooling that does not reduce operational load.

## When to use

- Audience is system admins, sysadmins, or hands-on infrastructure operators
- Flows involving patching, inventory, compliance findings, golden images, or host remediation
- When the primary actor is host/OS ops rather than cluster or platform engineering
